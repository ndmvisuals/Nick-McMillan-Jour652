10.  Why do we use media queries?

We use media queries to make a webpage look good on any device. Media queries
allow us to make out design responsive, and respond to rules that we give it, for example making
text smaller when a browser's width reaches a certain size.


11. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

Serif fonts have a decorative stroke at the end of letter stems and sans-serif
fonts do not. Sans-serif has a modern feel and is mostly used on web, while Serif
envokes a sense of luxury and antiquity.
